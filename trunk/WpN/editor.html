WpN

pronounced whuppin' or weapon

 

A generalization of the Oulipo w ± n algorithm.

Consider,
for any ordered set of tokens (Template Text),
what words you will keep (Static Text),
and what new words you will use (Input Text).
Then Edit.

 
 
 

Default Template Text from "The Sonnets" by Shakespeare; default Input Text from "Unknown Pleasures" by Joy Division.
